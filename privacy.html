🔐 Privacy Policy

📅 Last Updated: June 28, 2026

Pohyar AI is your all-in-one AI study companion — built for students and teachers who want to learn faster, create exam papers in minutes, and study smarter with artificial intelligence.

We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

a) Information You Provide Voluntarily

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and profile information when you register.
  • User Content: Quiz questions, exam papers, assignments, chat messages, and scanned questions you submit.
  • Syllabus Data: Course materials and syllabus content you upload for exam paper generation.

b) Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: App features used, session duration, frequency of use.
  • Camera Access: When you use Scan & Solve to capture question photos.
  • Storage Access: When you save assignments and exam papers as PDFs.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Generate AI-powered quizzes, exam papers, assignments, and translations
  • Provide instant solutions through Scan & Solve feature
  • Improve app performance and AI model accuracy
  • Send important updates about app features and services
  • Respond to your feedback and support requests

3. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

Pohyar AI uses third-party AI services to power its features:

  • Google Gemini — Powers AI Tutor, AI Chat, and content generation features
  • Groq / Llama — Supports AI-powered responses and content creation
  • PDF Export — For generating downloadable assignments and exam papers

These third-party services process your content (quizzes, questions, scanned images) to generate AI responses. We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties.

4. AI Processing Disclosure

Pohyar AI uses advanced AI models (Google Gemini and Groq/Llama) to:

  • Generate quizzes, exam papers, and assignments from your inputs
  • Provide answers to study questions
  • Translate text between 100+ languages including Urdu

Your inputs are processed by these AI services solely to generate the requested outputs. We do not use your data to train AI models without your explicit consent.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Secure Storage: Your data is stored on secure servers with encryption.
  • Data Retention: We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide services.
  • User Control: You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your data.
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request account and data deletion.
  • Opt-out: Disable data collection for non-essential features.

7. Permissions We Use

Pohyar AI requests the following permissions:

  • Camera: To capture questions via Scan & Solve.
  • Storage: To save PDF exports of assignments and exam papers.
  • Internet: To connect to AI services and generate content.

8. Children's Privacy

Pohyar AI is designed for students and teachers. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.
